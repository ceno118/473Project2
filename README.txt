The goal of this game is to move through the shoothouse as quickly as possible, finding your
way through the maze and shooting all targets.

Controls:

Character:
-Forward: UP
-Backward: DOWN
-Turn Left: LEFT
-Turn Right: RIGHT
-Fire: LEFT CLICK

Camera SELECTION:
-First Person: 1
-Birdseye: 2
-Third Person: 3

Post-Processing / Lights / HUD:
-NVGs: G
-Normal Light: N
-Toggle Flashlight: F
-Toggle HUD: H
